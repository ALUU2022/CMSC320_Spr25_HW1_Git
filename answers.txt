Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: To collaborate on projects with multiple people, from different places.  Seems like a good way to keep track of changes, have backups, and ways for people to iterate on a product.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Healthcare.  Data from patients is stored and retrieved in health databases, so healthcare professionals can reference the data and make informed clinical decisions.
